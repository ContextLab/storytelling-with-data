Part II

Part II
Independent Data Stories
Pitch, refine, critique — produce 3+ data stories on topics of your choosing

In Part II, students repeat three general steps in the storytelling process (video introduction):

  1. Pitching & brainstorming. Present ideas, form groups, workshop story ideas.
  2. Refinement. Workshop ideas and code. Bring up new content to learn.
  3. Critiquing. Discuss stories as a class with constructive feedback.

Each data story should follow the project template and include a README, YouTube video, notebook, and data files.
